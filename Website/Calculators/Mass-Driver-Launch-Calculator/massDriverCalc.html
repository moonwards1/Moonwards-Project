Lunar Mass Driver Launch Calculator

An equatorial mass driver accelerates a payload west→east (prograde) and releases it horizontally from a track elevated above the ground (25 m by default). The Moon's eastward rotation (about 4.6 m/s) adds to the release speed. Set a release speed, or tick the box to set a target apoapsis instead. Below orbital speed the payload arcs down from the release height and lands downrange.
