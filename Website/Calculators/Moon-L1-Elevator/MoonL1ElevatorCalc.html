Moon–L1 Space Elevator Calculator

A tether rising from the Moon's surface along the Earth–Moon line, through the Earth–Moon L1 point, continuing beyond it and terminating in a counterweight. The cable is sized so that with its full cargo load on it the stress everywhere equals strength ÷ safety factor. Because the counterweight needed depends on how much cargo rides the cable, this is a two-step tool: set the structure and traffic first, read off the minimum counterweight, then choose a counterweight at or above it.
