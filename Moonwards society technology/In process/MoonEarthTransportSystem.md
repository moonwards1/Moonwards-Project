# The cargo journey from the moon to the Earth's surface

## A Summary of Manufacturing

There are two industrial centers on the moon's surface, and one in lunar orbit at the Skyhook's industrial zone

### Equatorial manufacturing

- First site of development on the moon

- Focussed on production of the things needed in the early days
  
  - Fuel for the shuttles and the skyhook - LOX and magnesium powder
  
  - Silica fiber for the initial skyhook, before production of the best quality silica moved to Lalande and the skyhook.
  
  - Solar panels, flywheel energy storage, rocket components, robot components
    
    - Initially finished with a few components from Earth beyond local manufacturing capability, focussing on 'good enough' products
  
  - Construction materials for simple factory spaces and the structural bulk of the mass driver

### Lalande mining and manufacturing

- Second development site, with investment for demanding industrial capabilities and capacity
  
  - REE products, especially superconductors
  
  - Alloys, cermets, metal matrix composites, ceramics, specialty coatings, ultra-high purity billets
  
  - Produces the inputs used for final refining and manufacturing on the skyhook

- Rail line takes products from Lalande to the warehouses and loading platforms of the equatorial mass driver

### Skyhook manufacturing

- Third development site, that serves multiple functions, and is the site of the most demanding industrial processes. 
  
  - Site of mature silica fiber manufacture - drawing fiber from ultra-pure silica billets from Lalande, using the microgravity environment to draw strands 500 m long at ideal speed, in an environment that precisely controls cooling
  - Site of end-to-end production of all carbon-containing materials - mostly CNT, graphene, and silicon carbide. 
  - Specialty processing using the following techniques: chemical and physical vapor deposition, vacuum distillation and differential crystallization, magnetically confined plasmas, processing at very high or very low temperatures for extended periods, super-fast quenching (of limited quantities), large-scale and high-speed centrifugal processing for long periods.

- The manufacturing zone is on the outside of the orbital habitat, located some kilometers below the CoM, where it acts as the couterweight keeping the CoM at the right altitude, despite the length of the upper tether and the presence of significant mass at its top.

## Mass Driver

The mass driver is a structure 41 km long, sitting directly on the moon's equator, north-west of Lalande crater. The first kilometer, on the west end, is comprised of the warehouses, loading areas, and maintenance and repair facilities. The other 40 km is the length of the tracks.
