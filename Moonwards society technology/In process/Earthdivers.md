# Earthdiver capsules delivering cargo to Earth's surface

Earthdivers are single-use capsules manufactured in the industrial facilities located on the lunar skyhook. They are made of superior aerospace alloys, coatings, cermets,  and metal-matrix ceramics with properties that come from the unique manufacturing capacities of the moon's industry.  

- designed for a ballistic entry with enough lift to do up to 1000 km of cross-range travel before reaching the ground 
  
  - L/D is about 0.3

- arrive at one of 6 pairs of landing zones - see full arch
  
  - Baja California
  
  - Peruvian coast
  
  - Mauritania
  
  - Namibia
  
  - Horn of Africa
  
  - Australia
